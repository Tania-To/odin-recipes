Crepes recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g of all purpose flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 Liter of milk
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • a pinch of salt
  • 50g of melted butter

    • Direction

    Step 1

    Mix flour, salt and sugar in a big bowl.

    Step 2

    make a well in the middle of the flour, and add the 4 eggs

    Step 3

    Mix slowly until the mixture become hard and add the milk little by little

    Step 4

    When everything is mixed together, add the melted butter and mix again. Make sure the butter is cooled down

    Step 5

    Preheat a non-stick cooking pan and cook your crepes little by little
