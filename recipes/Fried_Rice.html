Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked white rice
  • tablesppon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

    • Directions

    Step 1

    Assemble Ingredients

    Step 2

    Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander

    Step 3

    Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stirl in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetable

    Step 4

    Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again
